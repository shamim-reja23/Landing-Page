Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MentorBot.

How is MentorBot different from other AI assistants?

Unlike general AI assistants, MentorBot is specifically designed for personal and professional development. It combines AI technology with proven mentoring methodologies to provide guidance that's tailored to your specific goals and learning style.

Can MentorBot replace a human mentor?

MentorBot complements rather than replaces human mentorship. It provides consistent, on-demand guidance and practice opportunities while supporting the relationships you have with human mentors and coaches.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. Your conversations and personal information are encrypted and never shared with third parties. You can delete your data at any time from your account settings.

What if I want to cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. If you cancel during your free trial period, you won't be charged.

Does MentorBot work on all devices?

Yes, MentorBot is accessible via web browser on any device including desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. We also offer native mobile apps for iOS and Android.
